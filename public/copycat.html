Copycat

Enter a Game ID of your choice and one of the three options considering the rules below. If a player has started game with that ID is in session, you will be matched with them. Otherwise, you will start a new game and someone else can enter your games ID to play against you.

Rules

If you are starting a game, you will make a 5x5 pixel art drawing for the other player to copy.
If you are joining a game, you will be the one trying to copy the first player's image.
Failing to copy the image or at least get close to it will result in a life being lost for the group.
You have three lives and your goal is to reach the highest round. Good luck!
